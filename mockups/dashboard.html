Open School-Climate Data Layer

CC BY 4.0 Daily refresh

A clean, school-level, EMIS-keyed climate exposure dataset. Built so other school operators, researchers, ministries, and aid agencies can layer their own analytics on top — without each having to wire up ERA5, MODIS, Sentinel-5P, and CAMS themselves.
